About Petstapost

Researchers all over the world have produced findings detailing why looking at social networking sites can make you feel depressed (TIME and The New Yorker). Too much use can cause loneliness, worry, and envy. But why do we continue to use them? Because we also love being connected, we're bored and we need breaks. Additional research has shown that looking at pictures of cute animals, rather than our friends' happy moments, can actually increase our productivity (Washington Post and Forbes).

Petstapost is a way to mix together the connection with friends and the photos that make us happier.



Our Purpose

A pet is abused every 60 seconds. Rescue organizations on a national and local level are saving those pets every day by getting them into foster care and shelters. In addition, each year 7.6 million pets enter animal shelters nationwide.

However, 31 percent of dogs and 41 percent of cats that enter animal shelters are euthanized because there is not enough space for all of the animals that need homes, and many shelters cannot afford to treat even minor health issues. Adopting a pet opens up space and other resources in shelters for more pets to be saved and find a safe, happy, forever home.

Petstapost donates 100% of all profits to organizations that save abused, homeless and sick animals that deserve a chance, including the Humane Society, the ASPCA, and our local organization Austin Pets Alive!.
